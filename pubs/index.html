About LLMIR and LLMIR Components

  • Optimizing KV Cache Management Through LLMIR - Chen Xingqiang, Zhou Mei, Wang Xin, Jiang Hao - In Proceedings of the International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML) - pp. 354-368 - July 12, 2024.
